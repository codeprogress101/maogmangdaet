Crash of a fairchild F27 (Air Manila) in Daet, Camarines Norte, September 6, 1973

During the takeoff roll on runway 06, while at a speed of 60 knots, the crew encountered technical problems with the nose wheel steering system. The airplane deviated to the right, veered off runway and came to rest against a pile of gravel. All 24 occupants escaped uninjured while the aircraft was damaged beyond repair.
